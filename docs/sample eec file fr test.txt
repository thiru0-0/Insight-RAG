Easwari Engineering College was established in 1996 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. 
The college is affiliated with Anna University and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

The Computer Science and Engineering department focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity.

The campus includes advanced laboratories, research centers, and incubation facilities for startups.

The institution regularly organizes technical symposiums, hackathons, and industry collaboration programs.

The library contains over 50,000 books, journals, and digital learning resources for students.
